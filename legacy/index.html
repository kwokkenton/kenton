About me

Hiya! My name's Kenton and I'm born and raised in Hong Kong (香港), though now studying in the United Kingdom. I like to think of myself as a person with a few different but interconnected interests, scroll down to find out more!

I am a scientist

I am a first year undergraduate physics student at Imperial College London who lives and breathes the spirit of science. Knowledge is liberating and I deeply believe that understanding the fundamentals of nature will open the path towards inventing the future.
My interests include

  • Biophysics
  • Computational physics
  • Data science
  • Machine learning

These are some selected projects of mine that I am particularly proud of!

  • Analysing and Evaluating Variational Quantum Monte Carlo Methods to determine the Ground State Energy of Helium
  • Construction of an Analogue Computer to simulate a Car Suspension
  • Shaking Away Catastrophe - An Investigation into the Pendulum Tuned Mass Damper
